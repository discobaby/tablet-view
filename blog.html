Ben's Britney Blog: My own Britney opinions :)

The Significance of the Blackout Cover

The 'Blackout' album cover has always been one of my favorites. For starters, the black hair is something that we have never seen from Britney.. and I love it. It is so edgy and fits the vibe of the album completely. The pink dress that was originally white, but photoshopped has become one of her most iconic looks. It coincides with the white fedora so well, which she is keeping on her head. The mess in the background, where you can see her reflection but it is covered up by the orange squares just shows that there is so much going on, almost too much- like a BLACKOUT.

Britney's Best Hairstyles

Britney has always been a versatile performer, but many do not know about how her hair is also versatile. She has the rare ability to look good in just about every kind of hairstyle that you can imagine. If you can think of it, she can look good in it. Personally, my favorite of her hairstyles is her curly hair looks. She used to do these back in the "Oops.. I Did It Again!" era and "Britney" (2001) era. I miss when she used to do it, but now she has a very distinct look with her straigtened hair. Another one of my favorites is from the 90s when she used to do a double pony- tail look. She did not have quite pigtails, so it was so interesting. She always wore pink glasses too with this look.

Britney's Iconic Shirts

Another thing that Britney is, is ahead of her time. She has so many looks that she wore in the early 2000s that people now, in the year 2021, are obsessed with and copying. She was the queen of the crop, a style that has come back into trend and has all of the peopleon TikTok shaking. Britney has had some of the most iconic ones to date, including my 2021 halloween costume the "sexsi" tank top and the "DUMP HIM" shirt. I've seen people trying to buy these all over the Internet, they have been sold out from shops for months. No one can get their hands on them. The way it is two decades later and people are still circulating back to her fashion is remarkable. Even if she does not release music again, she will forever remain iconic and revelant in the mainstream.
