Ben's Britney Mix: Welcome to the deep cuts :)

LISTEN TO MY TOP BRITNEY TRACK OF THE MONTH! :)

LISTEN TO MY FAV BRITNEY LOVE SONGS :)

There is just something different about early Britney love songs. They hit harder than any other songs for me. Most of Britney's early music is about love songs, and they are really special to me. When I was living alone last year during the height of the pandemic, I would listen to these songs and it would make me feel like I had somebody- even if they drive me crazy.

LISTEN TO MY FAV BADD B*TCH BRITNEY SONGS :)

These songs aren't "bad" at all, they are badd songs. They make you feel like a BO$$. They transcend you to another planet. They have this effect where you cannot stop strutting down the street, imagining you are Naomi Campbell on the runway. They infect your mind with main character disease, they delude your thoughts into convincing yourself that you are a popstar.

LISTEN TO MY FAVORITE *ADULT* BRITNEY SONGS

When Britney was finally able to start making more grown-up music, that is when her career took a turn. A turn for the best, her most iconic songs are about sex. "3", "Womanizer," "If U Seek Amy," most of her number one hits are all about it. She really has such a way with worrds and her voice is sexy, so why wouldn't she make bops about sex?

BEN LOVES BRITNEY: THE ORGINAL PLAYLIST

Here it is. The playlist that started it all. My very first Britney playlist, and I loaded all of my fav tracks onto here. I remember the day I made this, I was a senior in highschool and it marked my departure from being the boy obsessed with Lady Gaga and onto the boy obsessed with Britney Spears (and lady gaga, but since it was an older obsession everbody was sick of it.)

BRIT SONGS THAT TOOK ME A WHILE TO LIKE (BUT NOW I LOVE)

I acknowledge my flaws. I totally was not ahead of my time the first time I heard these tracks. However, these tracks really have become some of my favorite songs ever. Like wine, they age with time and they grow on you. I think that people need to step out of their comfort zones with music because you might find something you will like, even if it is by one of your favorite artists.

INSPIRED BY THE LEGENDARY MS BRITNEY SPEARS

I am a pop music critic. It consumes my life, somedays it is all I do. Just lay back and listen to all the new pop music that's been released. Friday's are so busy! That's new music release day for the US. Anyways, with all this pop music listening that I do, it is easy to recognize when Britney is inspiring new songs. She has such a signature beat, signature kind of lyric compsition, and it influences most pop girls to this day! Everyone from Gaga to Adele.
